A **sink** is anything that receives a wide event and does something terminal with it: ship to a provider, write to disk, send to a queue, post to a webhook. evlog ships built-in adapters for popular providers ([Adapters overview](/adapters/overview)) — this section is for everything beyond that.

You touch sinks when:

- You want to send events to a backend that has no built-in adapter
- You want to combine several drains with batching, retry, and fanout
- You want receivers to identify evlog traffic by `User-Agent` / `X-Evlog-Source`
